About Me

I'm a machine learning engineer at DCube Tech in Islamabad, where I work on Large language models to create custom chatbot.

At Dcube i am working on retrieval augmented generation (RAG) based chatbot. Also working on a chatbot for multi-user multiple document engine. Lastly, working on an end-to-end system for structural prediction of the payload for a API calls request.

Previously i have worked at PackageX as a Data Scientist, and worked on the pipeline for Named Entity recognition. I have also worked as a Research Assistant at NCRA-RDDL and worked on applying traditional machine learning techniques on a wide variety of problems. I also have a year of teaching experience as a Teachers Assistant at NUST-EME, in the department of Mechatronics. Have a year of management experience at CBL as the Management Associate of the Mechanical Workshop department of the company.
